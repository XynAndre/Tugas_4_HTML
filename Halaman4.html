Dovizioso: the attendance king

The Italian, along with teammate Jorge Lorenzo, has been competing on the World stage for 16 straight years - the third highest in the grid behind Valentino Rossi (Movistar Yamaha MotoGP) and Dani Pedrosa (Repsol Honda Team). It's huge testament to the 32-year-old, who in the last two years has been - on paper - Marc Marquez' (Repsol Honda Team) most consistent challenger.
